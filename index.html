Flux

an energy-based sliding puzzle

energy

manage growth. prevent collapse.

how to play flux

slide tiles using arrow keys. merge tiles of the same value to grow them. each move consumes energy. merges restore energy (+2 per merge). avoid running out of energy!

  • move: arrow keys
  • combine: merge same-value tiles
  • energy: moves cost 0.5, merges gain 2
  • goal: grow tiles without losing all energy
